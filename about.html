Zixuan Wang is a new media artist and game designer based in Chicago, graduating from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago in late 2025. His practice explores the porous boundaries between the digital and physical worlds. By integrating a variety of media and embracing a digitally-native approach to creation, he investigates the expansive possibilities that emerging technologies bring to artistic expression.
